Kheer for the Soul

Whenever I feel crestfallen about something or other in Rotary I seem to stumble upon the feelings of others. Our Hyderabad District Correspondent sent in this note - A doyen of Information Technology revolution in India had this to say about Rotary:
Mr. Ramalinga Raju of Satyam Computers Ltd., says "Rotary epitomized globalization long before it had b ecome a byword and fashionable. RI's approach to voluntary work and philanthropy by persons in business and vocation is the forerunner to the concept of 'Corporate Social Responsibility' on which so much emphasis is now laid. While a lot of importance is given to knowledge and technology, distributive justice and eradication of poverty will not happen unless the wealthy reach out with their time rather than money, and again in this, Rotary shows the way."

Reported by Rtn. Annapurna Sastry,
RC Hyderabad, RI District 3150

Elections Ahoy !

Elections continue to excite my fellow Indians whether it be the general election for electing the parliamentarians, or election to a committee to nominate a Rotary governor. The role of the media is always suspect in these times even though the undersigned belongs to this fraternity. To illustrate the point, a news item was sent in by a well-intentioned Rotarian. The banner headlines read "Rotary Governor election" and goes on to name the Rotarian with the photograph as having been elected to the nominating committee for the Governor. Further the report goes on to state the number of votes polled by the candidate which one believes is not to be revealed. In future we look forward to reporting the list of candidates who have filed their candidature as candidates for the election to the nominating committee of the Rotary governors, and getting this reported in the media.

Dear Mr Prannoy Roy, YOU and NDTV are missing out a great opportunity !!
